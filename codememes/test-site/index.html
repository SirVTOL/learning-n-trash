Dolls:

  • Ten little Dolls went out to dine; One choked her little self and then there were nine.
  • Nine little Dolls sat up very late; One overslept herself and then there were eight.
  • Eight little Dolls travelling in Devon; One said she'd stay there and then there were seven.
  • Seven little Dolls chopping up sticks; One chopped herself in halves and then there were six.
  • Six little Dolls playing with a hive; A bumblebee stung one and then there were five.
  • Five little Dolls going in for law; One got in Chancery and then there were four.
  • Four little Dolls going out to sea; A red herring swallowed one and then there were three.
  • Three little Dolls walking in the zoo; A big bear hugged one and then there were two.
  • Two little Dolls sitting in the sun; One got frizzled up and then there was one.
  • One little Doll left all alone; She went out and hanged herself and then there were none.
